# Grill Me

## Interview

- Resolve the decision tree one question at a time.
- For each question, provide one recommended answer and at least two alternatives.
- Explore the repository instead of asking questions that the code can answer.
- Continue until dependencies, tradeoffs, and acceptance criteria are explicit.

## Conclusion

Summarize the agreed decisions and ask whether to:

1. Implement the plan.
2. Export it to `.codex/grill/<name>.md`.
